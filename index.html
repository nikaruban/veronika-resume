Our Story

Founded in the heart of the city, Antique Mall has been a treasure trove for collectors and history enthusiasts for over 50 years. Our store is home to a curated selection of rare antiques, vintage finds, and timeless pieces, each with a story to tell.

We believe that every piece holds a piece of history, and we take pride in connecting our customers with unique and meaningful artifacts. Step into our store and embark on a journey through time.
